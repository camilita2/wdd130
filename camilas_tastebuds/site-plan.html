Home page

What is this website about?

Welcome to my opinion on food. This is a safe space to get to know about interesting types of food I have tasted so far in rexburg. I also post these food ratings so you guys, most likely if you are international, can relate to some of the opinions I have. Hopefully this will encourage you to go out more and meet new restaurants. I hope you enjoy this little rant I have made for you all.

Feedback

I would love for you guys to leave your opinions on food here. I would also appreciate it if you leave some good places you think I should try or some American recipes.

Disclaimer

Please take into account that this outlook on this website is my personal opinion and how much they differ from my home country's food or other countries' food I have had the luck to try. In no way am I trying to offend anyone with my ratings.

Food ratings

Cooked Chicken with rice

TThis is one of the few home cooked meals I know how to do. I had to learn how to cooked here on rexburg, and lets just say I do a pretty awful job at it. I dont know how to properly season. The first thing I want to point out is that the texture of chicken here is diferent from what it is in my home country. I feel like chicken here is more chewy, but I do like it. On the other hand, I dont feel like I am truly qualified to rate the rice here because I dont really know how to cook it, but from what I have perceived, it is not that different. So far this is one of the best meals because I feel like its healthy and it became my comfort food. Rating 7/10

Instant Ramen

I am personally not a fan of this type of instant ramen. I think that this brand is very easy to over cook and then the noodles become all mussy. I also think that the soup base is kind of off comparing it to other soup bases from other brands. Even though the taste is not the best, I will give it credit because it gets you out of sticky situations and it is very cheap. In addition to this, I dont feel like ramen is a very healthy food, I would just use it if I have nothing left. My overall rating for ramen is a 6/10.

Soup from soup for you

The soup in soup for you is the best soup I have had in my life. It is very tasty and I feel very nutritious. The soup usually has a protein and you get prety full with a small portion. They change the menu everyday so you get to try all diferent types of soup. I would say it is a very afordable place to eat, you can get a small soup and a grilled cheese for about 8 dollars. Although it is delicious food, the place is just open from 10-2 weekdays. My overall rating for the soup is 10/10 My favorite place so far.
